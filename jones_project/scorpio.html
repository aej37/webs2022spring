The Death Card

The Death card is probably the most feared and misunderstood of all the cards in the Tarot deck. Relax! The Death card can be one of the most positive cards in the deck.Death shows a time of significant transformation, change, and transition. You need to transform yourself and clear away the old to bring in the new. Any change should be welcomed as a positive, cleansing, transformational force in your life. The death and clearing away of limiting factors can open the door to a broader, more satisfying experience of life.
